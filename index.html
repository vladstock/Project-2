ENSO dance is a dance-meditative practice that combines dance with the skill of being in the present moment.

Based on the basic dance movements of the Middle and Far East, self-regulation techniques and mindfulness practice, the ENSO dance program invites you to look into your own depth.

Classes include elements of free movement, oriental plastics, working with images of nature, meditation and are both health-improving and creative.

Drawing inspiration from the art of the ZEN tradition, the program bears its qualities: simplicity, minimalism, focus on the process, contemplative slowness.

ENSO dance is, in a sense, "Zen dance gymnastics" for women in the big city, which, through naturalness and meditative movement, helps to return to their own natural harmony.

Who is this program for?

possible for you if you

  1. Are sedentary and need restful physical activity
  2. Do you want to relax and unwind in a dance atmosphere with the benefit of body and soul
  3. Love the zen aesthetics of Chinese and Japanese culture
  4. Do you want to learn the movement and plasticity inherent in female and oriental dances
  5. Are prone to contemplation and meditative creativity
